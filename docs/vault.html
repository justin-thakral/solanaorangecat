$Meow Tokenomics

Meow has 9 decimals, resulting in a total supply of 15 billion tokens. However, I intend to ensure that the token supply never reaches its cap. To achieve this, I'll optimize a formula that balances the amount of Meow rewarded versus burned. Rewards will be based on an individual's staked Orange Cat tokens and the total number of stakers. As the number of stakers increases, each participant will earn a smaller portion of the total reward pool. For instance, in the first year, I aim to distribute 25% of the Meow supply, while implementing a 5% burn rate. I'll allocate 30% of the maximum token supply to stakers based on the formula:

Meow Reward = (Orange Cat Staked / Total Orange Cat Staked) * (30% * Maximum Supply)

To achieve the goal of distributing 90% of the tokens within 5 years, I'll adjust the earn rates accordingly. Once 90% supply is reached, the burn rate will be nearly equal to the earn rate, with inflation of maximum 1% a year.
